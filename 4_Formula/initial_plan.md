## 3. Acoustic Treatment (Critical for Courses)

In a small  room, "boxiness" and echo will ruin your audio.

* **Bass Traps:** Place acoustic foam cubes in the top corners of the room.
* **Wall Panels:** Install 2-inch thick acoustic panels on the "first reflection points" (the spots on the walls directly to your left and right when sitting at your recording desk).
* **Floor:** If the floor is hard, a thick rug is non-negotiable to stop sound bouncing between the floor and ceiling.
